    Welcome

    My name is Irina Jones. I am a professional award-winning hair stylist. I have graduated two hair styling schools and have been practicing ever since. I have many years of experience and a great and loyal clientele. I love the art of hair styling, and I invite you to experience my "magic touch," - art of precision I have mastered through lots of practice (and keep refining it, of course)!

    It is all about YOU and your hair. My primary goal is to make everything possible to highlight your personality and individuality and to make you look the way you have always wanted to. Nothing is impossible here, let it be a simple haircut, a Brazillian blowout or more complex ideas, there will always be a guarantee of high quality and top-notch performance. Once again, I am here to make you shine, not to stand between you and your imagination.
